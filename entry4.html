Hobbies can distract from having negative thoughts and hold focus to something external.

Fun Facts:
  1. A meta-analysis of five studies found that adults over 65 who had hobbies reported higher levels of happiness.
  2. One study found that making art lowered participants' cortisol levels, a hormonal marker of stress.
